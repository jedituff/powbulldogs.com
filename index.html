Generation Zero - 5 pups (3M/2F)

KKKoba is a large dog exceeding 60 pounds. Dagny is a mid-size bitch at around 41 pounds. Generally, we are looking to breed smaller APBTs but temperament trumps all other factors and these two animals were too special - they had to be crossed. Koba has demonstrated high intelligence levels and deep human connection. Dagny is always seeking human affection but also demonstrated a will to protect her own. Both have an on/off switch that is uncommon in high-drive dogs. We expect to get animals ready to do whatever is asked of them, because it is asked of them, and to know when to hit that switch.
